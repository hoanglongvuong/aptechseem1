How do I place an order through the RyanaCalenders Website?


You can order online at the Ryana website through the basic ordering steps.


Please refer to the details of each order step as follows:


1. Login to your account at RyanaCalenders:

Please login with your existing Ryana account or login via Facebook / Gmail / Google. In case you have not registered an account, you can select the line "Create an account " to register an account at Ryana.

Once completed, you can click on the word "Create account " to complete the registration process.



2. Search for products:

You can find the product in 2 ways

  • Type the product name into the search bar.
  • Search by category.



3. Add products and place an order:

Once you have found the desired product, please click on the image or product name to access the product details page, then:

  • Check product information: price, promotion information.
  • Add products and choose to buy.


4. Enter the shipping address:

After you have had enough products to put in the cart and continue with the following steps to order

  • Fill out delivery information (Home or Office)
  • Please use a discount code (if applicable)
  • Cash Payment Or Online Payment
  • You can check the order by clicking the order confirmation email
